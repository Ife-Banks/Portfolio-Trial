Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how this website (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses information collected from Instagram through the Instagram Basic Display API.

Information We Collect

We only access basic profile information and media content from our own Instagram account in order to display posts on this website. We do not collect or store any data from visitors.

How We Use Information

The information retrieved from Instagram is used solely to display Instagram content on this portfolio website. No information is shared, sold, or used for advertising.

Data Retention

We do not permanently store any Instagram data on our servers. All Instagram content is fetched dynamically and displayed in real-time.

Your Privacy

This website does not collect personal information from visitors. We respect your privacy and ensure no third-party tracking is implemented beyond Instagram’s own embedded content policies.
